# 1. Document Purpose

This document serves as the constitutional foundation of the ReelLegacy project.

Every technical decision, design decision, product feature, engineering contribution, AI-generated implementation, and future enhancement should align with the principles defined here.

If any future documentation conflicts with this document, this document takes precedence unless explicitly superseded by a newer approved version.

This document intentionally focuses on **why** ReelLegacy exists rather than **how** it will be implemented.

---

# 2. Vision Statement

To become the world's leading platform for transforming personal histories, professional journeys, and organizational milestones into beautiful, cinematic stories that preserve memories, celebrate achievements, and inspire future generations.

---

# 3. Mission Statement

ReelLegacy empowers individuals, families, professionals, organizations, and institutions to preserve and present meaningful life stories through AI-assisted cinematic storytelling.

Rather than creating ordinary slideshows or timelines, ReelLegacy helps users produce emotionally engaging documentaries that communicate identity, legacy, growth, and impact.

---

# 4. The Problem

People spend years building careers, relationships, businesses, families, and communities, yet most of those stories remain fragmented across resumes, photo albums, cloud storage, social media, and scattered documents.

Traditional resumes communicate facts but rarely communicate growth.

Photo albums preserve memories but rarely preserve context.

Video editors require technical expertise that many users do not possess.

There is currently no unified platform that intelligently transforms structured information and personal memories into compelling cinematic narratives.

---

# 5. The Solution

ReelLegacy is an AI-powered storytelling platform that converts structured information, media, documents, and memories into professionally narrated documentary-style films.

Users can create:

- Personal biographies
- Career documentaries
- Celebration of Life films
- Founder stories
- Company history documentaries
- Retirement tributes
- Graduation stories
- Anniversary films
- Family history documentaries

The platform combines AI with human editing, allowing users to maintain complete creative control over every story.

---

# 6. Product Philosophy

ReelLegacy is not a video editor.

It is not a slideshow generator.

It is not a resume builder.

It is a storytelling platform.

Every feature should support one central purpose:

> Helping people tell meaningful stories through cinematic experiences.

Technology exists to serve storytelling—not replace it.

Users remain the authors of their own stories.

AI exists to assist, organize, enhance, and accelerate creativity.

---

# 7. Core Product Pillars

## Story First

Every feature should improve storytelling.

---

## Human-Centered AI

AI assists but never overrides the user's voice or intentions.

---

## Emotional Design

Interfaces should feel elegant, calm, inspiring, and respectful.

---

## Creative Control

Users should always be able to review, edit, regenerate, or customize AI-generated content.

---

## Trust & Privacy

Users own their data, media, and stories.

---

## Long-Term Preservation

Stories should remain valuable for years—not just for one event.

# 11. Non-Goals

ReelLegacy is **not** intended to become:

- A general-purpose video editing suite
- A replacement for professional film production software
- A social media platform
- A cloud storage service
- A genealogy platform
- A resume builder
- A portfolio builder

Those capabilities may integrate with ReelLegacy but are not its primary purpose.

# 12. Core Design Principles

The interface should always be:

- Modern
- Elegant
- Minimal
- Professional
- Emotionally engaging
- Accessible
- Calm
- Highly readable
- Responsive
- Consistent

---

# 13. AI Principles

Artificial Intelligence should:

- Reduce manual work.
- Never fabricate facts.
- Preserve factual accuracy.
- Clearly distinguish AI-generated content from user-provided content.
- Encourage user review before publication.
- Explain important AI decisions where appropriate.
- Allow regeneration without losing previous versions.
- Adapt to different storytelling styles while remaining truthful.

AI is a creative collaborator—not the storyteller.

---

# 14. Storytelling Principles

Every story should strive to:

- Follow a logical narrative flow.
- Highlight meaningful milestones.
- Maintain emotional authenticity.
- Respect cultural differences.
- Respect sensitive life events.
- Balance visuals, narration, and pacing.
- Celebrate achievements without exaggeration.
- Preserve historical accuracy whenever possible.

Stories should feel timeless rather than trendy.

---

# 15. Engineering Principles

The platform should be:

- API-first
- Modular
- Scalable
- Event-driven where appropriate
- Secure by default
- Component-based
- Reusable
- Testable
- Accessible
- Performance-oriented

Business logic should remain independent of presentation.

Features should be loosely coupled to encourage future integrations and extensions.

### AI-First Development Workflow

ReelLegacy follows an AI-first software development workflow, where different AI platforms specialize in different stages of the software development lifecycle.

The official development workflow is:

- **Product Planning & Documentation:** ChatGPT
- **Frontend Development:** Google AI Studio
- **Backend Development:** Google Antigravity
- **Code Review & Refactoring:** GitHub Copilot (optional)
- **Human Collaboration:** Future contributors

Every AI assistant should focus only on its assigned responsibility.

Project documentation remains the single source of truth for all implementation decisions.

No AI tool should introduce undocumented features or architectural changes without explicit approval.

---

# 16. Relationship with CareerCanvas

CareerCanvas and ReelLegacy are complementary products within the IdeaCodex Labs ecosystem.

**CareerCanvas** serves as the structured source of professional identity.

**ReelLegacy** transforms structured data into cinematic stories.

The two products should communicate through documented APIs rather than shared business logic.

Examples include:

- CareerCanvas exporting career timelines to ReelLegacy.
- ReelLegacy embedding completed documentaries into CareerCanvas portfolios.
- Shared authentication.
- Shared media where appropriate.
- Shared design language.
- Shared AI context where beneficial.

Neither product should become dependent on the other for core functionality.
